# Secure Password Generator

![Password Generator](https://example.com/password-generator.png)

> "The true measure of a password's strength lies not in its complexity, but in its ability to safeguard your digital life."

Welcome to the Secure Password Generator, a powerful command-line tool designed to generate secure and robust passwords tailored to your specific needs. In the ever-evolving digital landscape, maintaining strong passwords is crucial to protect your online accounts and sensitive information from unauthorized access.

## Features

- **Length Customization**: Specify the desired length of your password, ensuring it meets your security requirements.
- **Character Set Selection**: Choose which character sets (lowercase, uppercase, numbers, and special characters) to include in your password, enhancing its strength and complexity.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive prompts guide you through the password generation process, ensuring a seamless experience.
- **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: Developed in Go, a powerful and efficient programming language, the Secure Password Generator is compatible with multiple operating systems.
